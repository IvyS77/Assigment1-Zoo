Giraffe

The giraffe is the tallest terrestrial mammal, renowned for its strikingly patterned coat and elongated neck. Its natural habitat spans the open savannahs, woodlands, and lightly forested regions of Africa, where its unique physiology allows it to reach the highest treetops for sustenance. Celebrated for its graceful stature, gentle demeanor, and remarkable adaptations.
